FOUR CORNERS OF THE WORLD LIBRARY

The Four-Corners-of-the-World database collects publications by Oriental Orthodox Christians over the past fifty years as they are (and were) used in European communities. This includes publications produced in Europe as well as in other parts of the world, in a large variety of languages. This database was set-up by the members of the project Rewriting Global Orthodoxy: Oriental Orthodox Christians in Europe, 1970-2020, and hosted by Radboud University, Nijmegen.
